Problem Statement:

Develop a program that calculates a student's grade based on their marks in five subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer.
Tutor Joe's

Input: Marks for each subject (out of 100).

Process:

Calculate the total and percentage.

Assign grades based on the percentage:

≥ 90%: Grade A

≥ 80%: Grade B

≥ 70%: Grade C

≥ 60%: Grade D

≥ 40%: Grade E

< 40%: Grade F

Output: Total marks, percentage, and grade.
