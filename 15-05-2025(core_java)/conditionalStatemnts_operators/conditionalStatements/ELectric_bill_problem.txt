Create a program to calculate the electricity bill based on the following criteria:

Input: Number of units consumed.

Process:

Rates:

First 50 units: ₹0.50/unit

Next 150 units: ₹0.75/unit

Next 250 units: ₹1.20/unit

Above 250 units: ₹1.50/unit

Add a 20% surcharge to the total bill.

Output: Total bill amount including surcharge
