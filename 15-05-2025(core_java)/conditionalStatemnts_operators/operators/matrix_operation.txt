Matrix Operations


Description: Write a program that performs matrix addition, subtraction,
and multiplication. The program should take two matrices as input and display
 the results of these operations



Input:
Matrix A:
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9

Matrix B:
9 8 7
6 5 4
3 2 1

Output:
Addition:
10 10 10
10 10 10
10 10 10

Subtraction:
-8 -6 -4
-2 0 2
4 6 8

Multiplication:
30 24 18
84 69 54
138 114 90
